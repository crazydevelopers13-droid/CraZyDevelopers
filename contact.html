Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
Most standard websites are delivered within 48–72 hours. More complex projects like e-commerce stores or portals may take 5–7 days. We always give you a clear timeline upfront.
What information do I need to provide?
Just share your business name, what you do, the pages you need, any logo or images, and your preferred contact details. We'll guide you through the rest.
How much does a website cost?
Our pricing is transparent and competitive. Costs vary based on the number of pages and features required. Contact us and we'll give you a fair, detailed quote with no hidden charges.
Will my website work on mobile phones?
Absolutely. Every single website we build is fully mobile-responsive — it will look great and function perfectly on any device, screen size, or browser.
Can I update my website after it's launched?
Yes! We provide ongoing support. You can contact us anytime for content updates, design changes, or new feature additions at reasonable costs.
What if I don't have a logo or images?
No problem. We can help design a logo and source high-quality, professional images for your website. Just let us know your requirements.
